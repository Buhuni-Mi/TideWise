Student-led coastal action platform

Small coastal actions. Lasting ocean impact.

TideWise is a simple student website based on SDG 14. It helps users learn about marine pollution, reduce waste, and join practical beach protection activities.

Explore practical action Try the simulator

Why this matters

Healthy oceans support wildlife, food, tourism, and coastal communities. When plastic and litter reach the sea, the damage spreads far beyond the beach.

TideWise focuses on action: cleanup, prevention, awareness, and personal responsibility.

Our main focus

Protect coastlines

Beach cleanups remove visible waste, encourage responsibility, and build stronger environmental habits in communities.

Reduce daily plastic use

Simple choices such as reusable bottles, bags, lunch containers, and better waste sorting can reduce marine pollution.

Inspire local action

Students can also help by sharing ideas, joining programmes, and encouraging cleaner habits at home and on campus.
